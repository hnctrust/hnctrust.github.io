Harinarayana Charitable Trust

Hari Narayana Charitable Trust (HNCT) is a Social Welfare Organization since 2015, has been working towards for the upliftment of the poor, down trodden, and under privileged in the society irrespective of caste. organizing various social service activities as in the fields of Education, Vocational or Skill Development Training, Health, Sanitation, Agricultural Development, Environmental issues, Human Rights issues inclusive of the Child Rights, Women’s rights, Dalit or Downtrodden People’s Rights, Different kinds of the Self-Employment and Self-Development and many other Development Activities for the welfare and development of the poor and needy people who are belonging to the social economic backward sections.

Our Vision

Our vision is of a world in which underprivileged and deprived older persons suffering from vision loss join the mainstream of the society which could cater to the holistic needs and aspirations of the community as a whole and show them the way forward, we hope your generosity will be pleased by the way of helping these target people to provide an opportunity to serve these target women and help in our activity.

Our Mission

Most of the target children are illiterate, drop-outs, non-admitted because of the illiteracy and ignorance and backwardness of their parents. These people do not know the value of education which is essential in the human life. In the rural areas the main problems are poverty and unemployment. The parents pay more attention and worry about tomorrows work for food to feed their children and the family members. Due to utter poverty, the parents prefer to engage their children in the labor work than to school. In our region the children are found mainly employed in the unorganized sector. These children are deprived of the basic requirements of human development. Due to the Child Labor, the children become victims and lose their individuality, dignity, future, prosperity etc. it is merely poverty, illiteracy, ignorance of the latest technology and advancement in this modern society.
