Vedas

The Vedas are a collection of hymns and other religious texts composed in India between about 1500 and 1000 BCE. It includes elements such as liturgical material as well as mythological accounts, poems, prayers, and formulas considered to be sacred by the Vedic religion. Read More

P

Puranas

The Puranas are ancient and very important religious texts belonging to the Hindu. These consist largely of narratives about the history of the universe and its numerous epochs or cycles from creation to destruction, genealogies of kings, sages etc. These texts also deal with Hindu cosmology and philosophy.

I

Itihasas

In Sanskrit, Itihasa means history. For Hindus, an Itihasa is a religious story that tells about what happened in the past. Itihasas are usually epic poems. The important itihasas to Hindus are the Ramayana and the Mahabharata. The original meaning of Itiihaasa had a more precise connotation than the word History.
